Bounding Boxes


a study of Chronophotography with digital, Computer Vision methods

Summary:

An exploration of how advancements in Computer Vision and digital methods can replicate analog photographic methods such as Chronophotography.

This experiment explores if the advanced tracking methods can develop interesting processes for 3D sculpting the movement through time.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------->

Materials :

  • Custom Object Tracking model (YOLO)
  • Processing code
  • 3D modelling
