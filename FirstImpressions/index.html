First Impressions


custom trained object detection model

Summary:

A mini experiment to demonstrate how to train a custom object detection model.

A lot of focus is put on machine learning models' intelligence and ability to easily replicate human tasks. However, in reality a model requires a great deal of human guidance.

Does the machine understand the nuance of Impressionist painting methods or is it a clever ruse to deceive the human eye?
