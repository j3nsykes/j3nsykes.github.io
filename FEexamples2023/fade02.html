Feedback Shield:

Often when working with both software and hardware it can be difficult to identify where mistakes might occur. You now have 3 areas to troubleshoot; the code, the circuit, the relationship between the two.

The Feedback Shield aims to provide visual feedback of these areas to help you troubleshoot your work. It sits on top of your Leonardo board, allowing you to connect wires and components directly through it.

The shield provideds colour coded feedback via square surface mounted LEDs that map to corresponding pins. for example the first LED maps to pin 2 and the last pin 13.

    If the signal is PWM the LED will be blue
    The value is represented by the brightness of the LED.

In the context of this task the LED mapped to pin 9 should be blue in colour and fade in brightness.
