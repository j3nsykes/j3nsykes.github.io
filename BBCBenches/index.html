To See The World

Differently, Listen


in collaboration with Mil Stricevic and the BBC

Summary:

To celebrate the 50th anniversary of BBC Radio 4, a public audio bench toured key city stations across the UK.

Bespoke audio content and hardware was embedded into the arms of benches, making it freely accessible to anyone with a pair of headphones.
